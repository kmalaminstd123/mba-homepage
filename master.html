Application & Admission procedure at a Glance

  • Step 01 Select your program: select any Master's from our wide variety of Specialization and submit the required documents via email to apply.
  • Step 02 Check Eligibility and Scholarship opportunity: The required documents submitted by candidates will be assessed by the Admission Committee to determine eligibility for the program along with the partial scholarship.
  • Step 03 Confirmation of Eligibility and Scholarship: An email confirmation will be sent regarding the decision of the admission committee.
  • Step 04 Get Offer Letter and Enrollment Invoice: Selected Candidates will receive an offer letter for the program applied, along with the invoice for the first payment.
  • Step 05 Make payment: Pay the invoice amount and wait for the confirmation. And upon confirmation, new participants profile will be activated on the portal within 24hrs.
  • Step 06 Receive Acceptance Letter: From the day of payment, participants will get the Acceptance Letter within 7-10 working days.
  • Step 07 Academic Orientation: The Virtual orientation will be conducted as soon as the enrollment is completed within 5 to 7 working days.
  • Step 08 Start the course: After orientation participants will be fully prepared to begin the program. Participants will also be connected with our team for any assistance.
  • Step 09 Graduation Requirements: To earn a degree participants must complete all ECT reserved for the program, pass on all the assignments and the ARP (Action Research Paper) with a minimum 2.5 cumulative GPA and fulfill all financial obligations.
  • Step 10 Certificate: Official Certificate and Transcript (Electronic and Printed version) shall be provided after successful completion of the program.
  • Step 11 Convocation: After successful completion of the program, graduates can attend the convocation ceremony which requires separate registration. Graduates are encouraged but it is not mandatory.

Programme Benefits

A MSSCM degree prepares participants for leadership roles in managing production and logistics processes. Through courses on procurement, operations management, and logistics, participant learn to optimize the flow of goods and services from suppliers to consumers. With Supply Chain Management becoming increasingly important in today’s interconnected global economy, a Masters of Science in Supply Chain Management degree is a valuable asset for professionals seeking to advance their careers.

MSHRM specialization focuses on developing individuals who can effectively manage organizational talent in a rapidly changing global environment. Graduates will have the skills and knowledge needed to lead HR departments, develop and implement policies and training programs, and ensure compliance with relevant laws and regulations. This degree can lead to a variety of careers in human resources, including HR manager, recruiter, compensation and benefits specialist, and employee relations specialist.

The MSESHM is designed to enhance professionals with the necessary skills to effectively manage environmental, health and safety issues within an organization. Graduates under this concentration can pursue careers in a range of industries including manufacturing, mining, construction, and healthcare, among others. They can also work in consulting firms or government agencies as environmental, health and safety specialists.

The MSPM is a graduate-level program that equip participants with advanced knowledge and skills in managing projects within various industries. By completing this specialization, graduates will be prepared for leadership roles in project management and be able to effectively plan, execute, and close projects while meeting objectives and deliverables. Career opportunities for graduates include project manager, program manager, project analyst, and project consultant roles.

The MSDRM program aims to equip professionals with the necessary skills and knowledge to effectively manage natural and man-made disasters. Graduates of this concentration can pursue careers in various industries, including government agencies, nonprofit organizations, private companies, and international development organizations. The program prepares participants to contribute to reducing the impact of disasters and building safer, more resilient communities.

This decline enhances participants with the knowledge, skills, and abilities to lead and manage teams effectively. Graduates of the specialization can pursue careers in various industries and roles, including human resources, project management, executive leadership, and consulting. It also provides a deep understanding of the principles of human motivation and behavior, enabling participants to help organizations achieve their strategic objectives through effective leadership and people management.

A MSBM is a graduate-level course focused on developing skills and knowledge in areas such as strategic planning, organizational management, financial management, marketing strategies, and leadership. Graduates under this specialization are equipped with critical thinking, problem-solving, and decision-making skills necessary to succeed in diverse industries, including finance, consulting, marketing, healthcare, government, and non-profits. This specialization can lead to various career opportunities such as CEOs, executive directors, project managers, business analysts, and consultants.
