Curriculum for DBA programme

A blend of advanced research, leadership, and practical impact.

Research Methodology

Quantitative and Qualitative [8 credits]: This course focuses on advanced techniques for analyzing quantitative empirical data, with a special emphasis on second-generation statistical approaches and their practical applications in research.

Philosophy

Process of Applied Business Research [8 credits]: This course introduces students to the fundamental principles and development process of managerial business research.

Experiments

Survey Design [8 credits]: This course provides students with an overview of methods and practices for creating and utilizing data based on experiments and surveys in research.

Scholarly

Conference/Seminar [16 credits]: This unit focuses on the attendance of students in different conference, seminars, and certifications etc in their field of study to enhance their knowledge and network with peers.

Doctoral

Thesis [56 credits]: This unit requires students to undertake in-depth research in their chosen field and produce a doctoral thesis that meets the programs requirements.

Viva

Defense [24 credits]: This final unit requires students to present and defend their doctoral thesis in a viva voce examination conducted by external examiners.

Role of the Supervisor

The designated supervisor’s role in the program is similar to that of a standard Doctorate Project supervisor. However, their focus is primarily on mentoring the candidate, providing support and guidance during the preparation of research material for examination (oral defense), and overseeing the examination process. The supervisor’s responsibilities include:

  • Assisting the candidate in the selection of suitable publications for inclusion in the submission
  • Providing guidance and advice to the candidate in relation to thesis preparation.
  • Advising the candidate on any necessary research training requirements.
  • Supporting the candidate through the examination (oral defense) process.

Practitioner’s Doctorate

Accelerate Your Expertise with the Practitioner’s Doctoral Pathway

Designed for visionary professionals who are ready to transform their experience into academic distinction, our Practitioner’s Doctorate Pathway offers a dynamic, career-aligned route to earning your doctorate. Ideal for seasoned experts with demanding careers, this program recognizes and builds upon your prior groundbreaking research, publications, books, or influential contributions to your field.

Who Should Apply?

This methodology is well-suited for individuals who has previous research work and understanding and experience in a particular professional area.

Entry and application criteria

  • Possession of a Master’s degree, preferably with a thesis component, from a recognized institution
  • Potential to meet the necessary criteria for obtaining a Doctorate degree.
  • A minimum of eight years of work experience, with at least three years in a managerial role.
  • Submission of a fully completed application form, along with the required documents.

Submission Requirement

  • Submit a stand-alone or compilation of research-based written work, which must be between a minimum of 20,000 words and a maximum of 25,000 words
  • Defend the submission in front of a panel of experts, which may be conducted online via video conferencing.
  • Demonstrate that the scholar has conducted a comprehensive and critical investigation of a specific area of research and made an independent and original contribution to the existing body of knowledge as well as professional practice.

Honorary Doctorate

Celebrate Legacy, Inspire Change: Honorary Doctorate Distinctions

Awarded to visionary leaders whose transformative contributions have reshaped industries, empowered communities, and driven global progress. This elite honor celebrates pioneers whose strategic leadership and innovative practices have left an enduring mark on the professional world. The esteemed Assessment Board rigorously evaluates candidates’ profiles, recognizing those whose expertise and dedication have elevated business practices on national and international stages.

Is a Doctorate Honorary Doctorate in any Discipline

Excellence knows no bounds. This distinction honors trailblazers—artists, scientists, educators, innovators, and changemakers—whose groundbreaking work transcends traditional boundaries. Whether through pioneering research, humanitarian service, cultural impact, or disruptive innovation, this accolade celebrates individuals who have redefined their fields and inspired generations. No prior academic coursework is required—only a legacy of extraordinary achievement and service to humanity.

Why Pursue an Honorary Doctorate?

  • Global Prestige: Join the ranks of global visionaries celebrated for their indelible impact.
  • No Coursework: Focus on what truly matters—your achievements speak for themselves.
  • Lifetime Recognition: Formalize your legacy with a title that reflects your lifelong dedication.
  • Universal Relevance: Open to leaders in any discipline, from tech and healthcare to arts and social justice.

Requirement

The incumbent must possess significant achievements in their academic and professional endeavors. And they must have made notable contributions to the development of their professional community.
