Course Introduction

The Master of Business Administration (M.B.A.) program is designed to equip you with essential modern management knowledge, skills and tools to ensure your mindset, skill set and application ability for the business growth and sustainability. MBA program accredited by European International University is one of the best in class in the global education. Our MBA Graduates are highly confident on the business management along with the subject matter socialization to contribute to the organization for its growth.

Corporates also appoint our MBA graduate with hoe and confidence for their professional skill set and business centric mentality. In this course you will study and learn modern tools and techniques on the particular subject along with business areas. The curriculum has been developed by a pool of industry expert on the particular area with their expert contribution in the respective field.

Course Modules

Introduction to environmental, health, and safety management principles, regulatory frameworks, and organizational culture.

Key Topics:
  • EHS Management Systems
  • Risk Management Fundamentals
  • Regulatory Compliance Overview
  • Organizational Safety Culture
  • Stakeholder Engagement
Assessment: Case study analysis, regulatory compliance project

Comprehensive study of environmental management systems, ISO 14001 implementation, and environmental impact assessment.

Key Topics:
  • ISO 14001 Standards
  • Environmental Impact Assessment
  • Waste Management Strategies
  • Water and Air Quality Management
  • Carbon Footprint Analysis
Assessment: EMS implementation project, environmental audit report

Advanced occupational health and safety management, including hazard identification, risk assessment, and safety program development.

Key Topics:
  • Hazard Identification & Risk Assessment
  • Occupational Health Programs
  • Safety Training & Communication
  • Incident Investigation & Analysis
  • Emergency Response Planning
Assessment: Safety program design, incident investigation case study

Integration of sustainability principles into business strategy and corporate social responsibility frameworks.

Key Topics:
  • Sustainable Business Practices
  • Corporate Social Responsibility
  • Life Cycle Assessment
  • Green Supply Chain Management
  • Sustainability Reporting
Assessment: Sustainability strategy development, CSR report analysis

Development of leadership skills and strategic thinking for EHS professionals in management roles.

Key Topics:
  • EHS Leadership Principles
  • Strategic Planning & Implementation
  • Change Management
  • Performance Measurement
  • Communication & Influence
Assessment: Leadership portfolio, strategic plan presentation

Independent research project applying EHS management principles to real-world organizational challenges.

Project Components:
  • Literature Review
  • Problem Identification & Analysis
  • Solution Development
  • Implementation Planning
  • Final Presentation
Assessment: Research dissertation, oral defense presentation

Frequently Asked Questions

The MSEHSM program is designed to be completed in 18-24 months, depending on your pace of study. As it's a self-paced program, you have the flexibility to accelerate or extend your timeline based on your personal and professional commitments.

Yes, the MSEHSM program is 100% online, allowing you to study from anywhere in the world. All lectures, assignments, and interactions are conducted through our advanced learning management system. However, you'll have access to live virtual sessions and one-on-one consultations with faculty.

Graduates can pursue various roles including EHS Manager, Environmental Consultant, Safety Director, Sustainability Manager, Risk Assessment Specialist, Compliance Officer, and Corporate Sustainability Advisor. The program also prepares you for senior leadership positions in EHS management.

No, there are no residency requirements for this program. All coursework, including the capstone project, can be completed online. However, we do offer optional networking events and workshops that you may choose to attend in person.

We provide comprehensive student support including academic advisors, technical support, library access, career counseling, and peer networking opportunities. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist with any questions or challenges you may encounter.

Yes, we accept transfer credits from accredited institutions. Up to 30% of the program credits may be transferred, subject to evaluation and approval. Our admissions team will review your previous coursework and provide a detailed credit transfer assessment.
